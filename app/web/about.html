What is LakeSignal?

An AI-powered news intelligence system that reads financial news in real time, identifies which stocks are affected, and predicts how prices might move — before the market reacts.

📡
Real-Time News Monitoring
Continuously scans RSS feeds from Yahoo Finance, MarketWatch, CNBC, Reuters, SEC filings, and Seeking Alpha every 5 minutes.
🧠
AI-Powered Analysis
Each news article is analyzed by Claude (Anthropic's AI) through Databricks Foundation Models to predict market impact with direction, magnitude, and confidence.
📊
S&P 500 Coverage
Tracks all 523 tickers in the S&P 500 universe. When news mentions a company, LakeSignal automatically maps it to the right stock ticker.
✅
Daily Proof & Backtesting
Every day, LakeSignal compares yesterday's predictions against actual stock prices. See exactly how accurate the AI was — full transparency.

How It Works

1
Ingest
News articles are fetched from 8 financial RSS feeds every 5 minutes and stored in a Delta Lake table.
2
Resolve
Each headline is scanned against 523 S&P 500 tickers to identify which companies are mentioned.
3
Score
An AI model reads the article and predicts: direction (up/down/neutral), magnitude (1-10), price move %, confidence, and risk tags.
4
Prove
Next day, actual stock prices are fetched and compared against predictions. Accuracy is tracked over time.
